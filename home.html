Our Story

Refreshed Skincare is a skin care and wellness brand that is committed
to creating effective, clean, and sustainable skincare items. Created by avid skincare and wellness advocates,
Refreshed Skincare brings their customers personalized skincare regimens,
and the right products to do so. In order to do this, the
brand supplies each customer/user the ability to take a skincare quiz.
This quiz will offer them the knowledge and understanding of their type of skin,
and what products within the brand best matches with their results. Refreshed Skincare brings
an innovative way to understand your skin, and what you are putting on it.
This brand brings a new twist to skincare, hiding no secrets, and supplying
you with the right resources to achieve the results that you want; using clean and pure
resources that won't make your skin feel heavy or irritated.

The skincare lines include cleansers, moisturizers, and body care.
The brand also offers a line of facial tools including reusable face cleansing pads.
The line is offered through a range of types of skin ranging from sensitive and light, to long-lasting and deeply-penetrating.
